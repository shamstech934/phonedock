# Data provenance policy

Every collected draft retains source ID/name/URL, collection and verification timestamps, provider record ID, normalized checksum, source reliability and field-level provenance/confidence. Raw credentials are never provenance. Conflicting values remain reviewable with old/new values, source, time and confidence. Unverified data must be labelled and cannot be represented as confirmed fact.

Manual edits take precedence unless an administrator explicitly accepts incoming changes. Preserve source records and audit history according to the retention policy and licensing agreement. Image provenance and usage rights require manual approval when unclear.
