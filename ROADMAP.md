# Roadmap

## Release-candidate exit criteria

- Production-like build and Playwright suites pass with configured services.
- Lighthouse and accessibility audits are captured for the required page set.
- Restore drill, rollback drill, alerts and cron monitoring are verified.
- Commercial admin/reporting gaps are either completed or explicitly removed from the 1.0 launch claim.
- Independent security review closes launch-blocking findings.

## Post-1.0

- Versioned public/mobile API with quotas and lifecycle policy.
- Durable distributed job orchestration and rate limiting at multi-instance scale.
- Affiliate/sponsor attribution and reporting with fraud controls.
- Newsletter campaign management and preference center.
- Data retention/archival automation and production-scale index telemetry.
- Continuous performance budgets and synthetic user-journey monitoring.

Roadmap items are not commitments until scoped, threat-modeled and assigned measurable acceptance criteria.
